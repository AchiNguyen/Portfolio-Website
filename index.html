Engineered at Vancouver Island University, a captivating rolling ball structure—crafted with popsicle sticks—integrates technology for interactive physics and engineering education. Arduino-controlled elements include an 8-bit MIDI player, IR ball counter, Archimedes staircase, Ultrasonic speed sensor, and LCD display.
