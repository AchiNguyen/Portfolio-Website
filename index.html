About Me

Hi there! My name is Nguyen, and I am currently a Chemical Engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I have always had a passion for technology and programming, and I am proud to call myself a self-taught coder. Throughout my academic career, I have been working on various projects that merge my love for chemical engineering and coding. I am based in Vancouver and, in my free time, I am also an avid soccer player. I find immense joy in the teamwork and strategic elements that come with the sport. This dual passion for technology and teamwork has significantly shaped me into a strong problem-solver and a collaborative team player. As I embark on my journey, I am actively seeking summer internships or jobs in the software engineering field. I am excited to continue learning and growing in my career, and I firmly believe that with my skills and dedication, I can make a valuable contribution to any team. Let's get connected
