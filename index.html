About Me

Hi there! My name is Achi, and I am currently an Engineering student at the University of British Columbia. I have always had a passion for technology and programming, and I am proud to call myself a self-taught coder. Throughout my academic career, I have been working on various projects that merge my love for chemical engineering and coding.
